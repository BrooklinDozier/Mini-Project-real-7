The Coral Reef Adventure

Environmental Protection About Coral

The Mission

The goal of this project is to develop an interactive and educational game that allows users to engage with a virtual coral reef system. This game aims to illustrate the impact of human influence, such as divers with sunscreen, plastic pollution, and temperature rise due to global warming, on coral reefs. Additionally, the game will highlight the cascading effects of these actions, leading to the loss of fish and marine life within the ecosystem. The project also aims to showcase the pristine beauty of coral reefs before human intervention, promoting awareness about the importance of coral reef conservation.
